Art journals

2017 - 2019
by Rio Cantre
Keeping a journal doesn't only help me dealing with depression and mental health and made me more creative. Because I'm not always traveling, I don't have the luxury to travel as much as possible. Mostly, I'm just at home watching travel videos and vlogs. So I need to do something with my journal. I don't want to use it only while traveling but also bringing out the inner creative child in me. I started using my journal like a canvas to make something artsy. Fellow journal keepers and planners also inspire me. I had to try it myself, and I came up with documenting my past travels, creative thoughts, and events. It helps me become more creative and open to sharing my thoughts. Spreading positivity to anyone who sees it.
