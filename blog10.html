Creative planner

2016 - 2018

Usually, my planner is not always full of appointments nor meetings, or exam dates. I'm just an average person with a typical day. But having a planner and always empty, it's depressing to look at. So I started to make it a bit catchy and creative. Some kept planners as part of their daily lives. I got inspired and created an ordinary day into a special one. Starting a planner is daunting at firstâ€”no idea what to plan nor how to get started. With the wide use of the internet, you can search for a community that can help you start making a creative planner. The wide range of journal keeping to creative planning brings fellow artists together. There is also Bujo and bullet journals which is similar to creative planning and journaling. You can always choose which one you prefer.
