Traveling with journal

2017 - 2019

My journal is my best friend. It helped me with my mental health and to stay creative. It also pushes me to travel and explore places I wanted to see. It's like magic! It brought me to beautiful places and gave me the courage and stay curious. Keeping a journal was a life-saver. With the everyday stress, reading my past travel journal makes me happy. I'm a homegirl and keeping a journal change my views about traveling. It helps me see the beautiful world out there, though I only visited a few. To me, it was a travel to remember. I wrote it down in my journal. Take photos as evidence. To remind me that I had been there once, and it was a secret of mine.
