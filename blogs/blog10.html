Creative planner

2016 - 2018
by Rio Cantre

Usually, my planner is not always full of appointments nor meetings, or exam dates. I'm just an average person with a typical day. But having a planner and always empty, it's depressing to look at. So I started to make it a bit catchy and creative. Some kept planners as part of their daily lives. I got inspired and created an ordinary day into a special one. Starting a planner is daunting at first—no idea what to plan nor how to get started. With the wide use of the internet, you can search for a community that can help you start making a creative planner. The wide range of journal keeping to creative planning brings fellow artists together. There is also Bujo and Bullet journals to creative planning and journaling. You can always choose which one you prefer.


Over the years, most planners have remained largely the same and most of the designs of day planners felt like they were stuck in the 80’s. No wonder many of us abandoned them and turned to our phones to replace them. Besides the cons of the phone planner, there is the basic appeal of tangible memories over technological memory . We miss the handmade feel; the creativity of doodling and journaling on paper. We place sentimental value on something when we’ve invested time and effort into creating it. Designing a completely original and personal snapshot of your daily life fosters a feeling of happiness and ownership more than any phone calendar could ever give.
