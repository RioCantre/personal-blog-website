Journal Doodle

2017 - 2018
by Rio Cantre

When I was younger, I love to draw. My inspiration was my father. I grew up seeing him draw botanicals, and as a kid, I dream that I, too, someday. That someday turns to doodle. I started out doodling with my journals. First, I got inspired by the community of INKTOBER. Inktober is a month-long art challenge created by artist Jake Parker focused on improving skills and developing positive drawing habits. Every day in October, anyone participating in the Inktober challenge creates an ink drawing and posts it online. I saw many excellent entries for their challenges, daily, monthly, and even yearly. With this, it awakens my inner child, who loves to draw. I started participating with the #inktober community and drew the characters I love. I drew some places I had visited. Eventually, I started doodling on my travel journal, showcasing my favorite footwear cause I wear it when I'm on vacation, even it's a short one. It's a get-away for me to be free temporarily and taste the sweet escape from reality in just a short period.

Doodled the comfy shoes ever purchased!
