Paris travel journal

2018
by Rio Cantre

One day, I thought to myself, "I want to be in Paris." So I did, I booked a flight and went to Paris for three days. It was my first time visiting. Within the three days, I had experienced a magical moment. So little knowledge of the language spoken, I had strolled the city with no issues. Every street was a masterpiece. People are hustling and wear headphones. I spend my first day with a friend to help me take photos to remember (I'm not photogenic) and stayed in a six female bedroom hostel and met a fellow traveler. We spent a night visiting a museum (though it's already closed) and ate ice cream. The next day, I was lucky enough that it was Sunday and the ticket to the museum of Musee d'Orsay was for free. It was one of the highlights of my visit. To finally visit the museum I admire. I was so happy that my stress was gone. Paris is such a beautiful country. On my last day, I went to see a consultant cause I needed help. My visit was eye-opening. The consultant helped me understand my problem with my mental health in general. I was so glad to meet her.
