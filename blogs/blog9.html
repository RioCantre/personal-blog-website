Asia travel journal

2018
by Rio Cantre

I grew up and was born in one of the islands of the Philippines. Being away from my motherland, I didn't realize I was homesick. I visited my homeland, but I didn't document it while visiting the neighboring countries. Visiting a country with little knowledge of the language was a challenge for me. But it was a fun experience at the same time. I traveled few Asian countries and documented it in my journal. I met a penfriend and discussed stories over food. Stroll the city and bid farewells. But my adventure has just started. My main objective was to be able to order a proper meal and eat it properly. I mean, I was primarily alone during my trip, and ordering food can be a challenge, and eating with strangers. I manage somehow. Extravagant as it is! You can't be poor when visiting Asian malls, and I became penniless after my visit. Though the tourist spot was always packed, planning was convenient before arriving at the tour stations.


The chaotic symphony that is backpacking Southeast Asia is one of the most fun and powerfully rewarding experiences any backpacker can have. If you are looking to embark on the adventure of a lifetime, a journey backpacking through Southeast Asia will certainly fulfill that dream beyond your wildest imagination .
